Research Projects

Vortex Dynamics in Spin-Imbalanced Fermi Gases

2024 - Present

Computational studies of vortex behavior in polarized superfluid systems using TDSLDA simulations on the LUMI supercomputer. Focus on understanding transverse forces and vortex trajectories in inhomogeneous Fermi superfluids.

Persistent Currents in Polarized Superfluids

2024 - Present

Investigation of persistent current phenomena in spin-imbalanced superfluid systems, including development of analysis tools for energy metrics and phase dynamics.

TDSLDA Simulation Framework

Ongoing

Development of Python workflows for analyzing time-dependent superfluid local density approximation simulations, including tools for data processing, visualization, and high-performance computing workflows.
