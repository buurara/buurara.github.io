About Me

I am a physicist at the Institute of Physics, Polish Academy of Sciences (IFT PAN), working on theoretical and computational studies of ultracold atomic gases and superfluid systems.

My research focuses on vortex dynamics, spin-imbalanced Fermi gases, and non-equilibrium phenomena in quantum many-body systems using computational methods including time-dependent superfluid local density approximation (TDSLDA).

Research Interests

  • Ultracold atomic gases and quantum many-body systems
  • Superfluid systems and vortex dynamics
  • Spin-imbalanced Fermi gases
  • Non-equilibrium phenomena in quantum systems
  • High-performance computational physics
